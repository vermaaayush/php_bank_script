Signing In

To sign in to Trexlore Bank Online Banking, enter your Username and Password.

During the sign in process, we may ask you to answer one of the Security Questions you set up previously.

If you need help signing in, please call us at:

1-800-488-BANK (2265), option 3

Monday through Friday, 4 a.m. – 10 p.m. Pacific time

Saturdays, Sundays and most holidays, 5 a.m. – 10 p.m. Pacific time
